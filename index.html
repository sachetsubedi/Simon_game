How to play
  1. 1. Watch carefully the sequence of the boxes being lit and remember the sequence.
  2. 2. Click on the boxes in the same sequence as they were lit.
  3. 3. If you click on the wrong box, the game will end and you will have to start over.
  4. 4. If you click on the right box, the game will continue with the next box in the sequence.
    After all the sequence is completed, the level is increased.
